# Introduction to Threaten Modeling

**Applications Architecture Overview**

Fronted, Backend, Db, Auth, APIs

**STRIDE Model(Identifying Threats)**

- Spoofing
- Replication
- Denial of Service
- Tampering
- Information Disclosure
- Elevation of Privilege

**Risk Mitigation Strategies** 

- Authentication, Authorization
- Input validation
- Rate Limiting and firewalls
- Data Protection
- Logging and Monitoring
